Discussion Board

  • Why is encryption important?
    Encryption ensures data security, confidentiality, and integrity. It protects sensitive information from unauthorized access.
    Source: NIST
  • Can encryption be misused?
    Yes, cybercriminals use encryption to hide illegal activities, such as ransomware attacks.
    Source: Europol
  • Discussion on real-life ethical concerns about encryption.
    Governments and tech companies debate the balance between privacy and security. Backdoors in encryption can weaken security for all users.
    Source: EFF
